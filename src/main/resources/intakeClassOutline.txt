SO

2 solenoids
1 retracts
1 extends

2 motors
1 left
1 right

When bumper LEFT is pressed/held
- Retract solenoids

When bumper RIGHT is pressed/held
- Extend solenoids

When trigger LEFT is pressed/held
- Power motors in;
    - Speed based exponentially on analog input of trigger

When trigger RIGHT is pressed/held
- Power motors out
    - Speed based exponentially on analog input of trigger
